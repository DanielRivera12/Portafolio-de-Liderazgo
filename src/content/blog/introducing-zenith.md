# Welcome to Zenith

Building a SaaS product is hard enough. Building the landing page shouldn't take another two weeks. 

We created Zenith to solve a problem we faced constantly: we needed a fast, beautiful, and high-converting landing page for our micro-SaaS projects, but we didn't want to start from scratch every time.

## Why Astro?

Astro is the perfect framework for content-heavy marketing sites. It ships zero JavaScript by default, resulting in incredibly fast load times. When you do need interactivity, you can easily drop in React, Vue, Svelte, or even just Vanilla JS.

### Key Benefits:

1.  **Blazing Fast:** Maximize your Lighthouse scores.
2.  **SEO Optimized:** Server-side rendering ensures search engines can index your content.
3.  **Component Driven:** Easy to customize and extend.

## What's included?

Zenith comes packed with sections tailored for SaaS products:

*   Hero with high-converting CTA
*   Feature highlights
*   Testimonial grids
*   Pricing tables with toggles
*   FAQ accordions
*   MDX-powered blog
